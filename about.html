About Me

My short life story.

My life has been quite different when compared to others. Born of Hispanic parents, I was living in Peru, my mother country, but soon after, my family decided to move to Japan and start a new life. I was only a year and a half when this happened. Since then, I had been going to school there, learning a language and living in a culture completely different from that of my family's. Althought this experience was difficult for them, it has been truly beneficial for me and has given me a unique life perspective.

Coming to the United States was another big change in my life. It had been one of my goals, but I knew it was going to be challenging because it meant I would have to adapt to a new culture and lifestyle once again. However, my experience here has been great so far meeting many good people from different parts of the country and the world.

I don't know where life will take me from here, but one thing I'm sure is that I have the power to decide in which direction I want to go. With so many opportunities and options, I am sure there must be a place where I can belong in this country.
